President

Nicolas Adami-Sampson

Nicolas Adami-Sampson, also known as Brother Low Road, is a junior studying Management Information Systems at WPI. Although Connecticut born, Nicolas is a Scot through and through. A member of Glee Club since his freshman year, he is also a member of the Vocal Performance Lab, the chamber group associated with Glee Club. Other groups he is a member include Vox Musical Theater, and Sound Logic, one of the all male a cappella groups on campus. He is in a contstant state of awe.

Jeremy has been an avid musician since his early childhood, performing everything from show tunes to rock n' roll. When he's not solving calculus or chemistry problems, he can be found singing in the many choral groups on campus, including Simple Harmonic Motion, Men's Glee Club, Festival Chorus and Vocal Performance Lab.
