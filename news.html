The Great Redesign

In the begining, there was the OG website. It was incredibly static, but nobody thought we'd ever add stuff to it or make any major changes, and if we did make any changes the orignal creators would have graduated by then and therefore become someone else's problem, and so it was good. We even got really good search rankings... somehow. Anyway, eventaully shit got real, and people realized that not being able to actaully change anything kinda sucked, and that sidebars in general were pretty lame, and using static images for navigation was truly one of the stupidest ideas ever, and that the website in general just kinda looked old, and not befiting that of an engineering school. And so a lone noob made everythign better with modern HTML5, CSS3, sweet sweet JavaScript, (plus a heavy dose of Bootstrap, a teeny bit of piracy, and a lot of late nights). Eventually, a decent looking site emerged. This is his story...
