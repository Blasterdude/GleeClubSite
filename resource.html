Glee Club Constitution

This is our constitution, which is reviewed annually and typically updated with new amendments at scheduled intervals twice a year, though ammendments may be proposed at any point throughout the year. Amendments are proposed during open officer meettings by either officers themsleves or general members attending the meeting. Amendments are voted on by the board, and if approved are voted on again for approval by the general body at the next rehearsal.
